About Us

They will bring a unique gamified staking to the holders NFTs, with a unique utility token $DUDZ, which will be the lifeline of Solgwais Ecosystem, while offering many different types of utilities and oppurtunites with our web2 and web3 partners.

Solgwais main goal is to become a community philanthropy hub on the Solana Blockchain, using the power of a community and the founders vision to connect and give back to those in need through Web3 and Blockchain Technology. The Legend of the SOLGWAIS

Long ago, in a mythical land shrouded in mystery and magic, there existed a great power at the core of the world. It was an immense, glowing orb of pure energy that pulsated with life and creation. From this source of power, the Solgwais were born. They were creatures of magic, created to guard and protect the land from any who would seek to abuse the great power at the core of the world.

The first Solgwais were known as the Dreamweavers, and they possessed the power to control the elements of fire, water, earth, and air. They named the source, “Aurumana” , “Aurue”(ah-rue) for short. They were swore protectors of Aurue. They were the only race of Solgwais, but as time passed, the power at the core of the world began to change them as they ventured off, giving rise to different races with unique abilities and powers.

As the Solgwais explored their new world, they began to form different cultures and civilizations around each race. There were the Oakenthorns or Forest Dwellers, who had an affinity for nature and the ability to communicate with animals. There were the Emberfall Solgwais or Fire-Bearers, who could control flames and harness the power of the sun. There were the Water-Bearers or Seaform Solgwais, who could manipulate water and summon powerful storms. Many other races walked the land but the Sky-Travelers stood out, as they had the gift of flight and control over the winds.

The different Solgwais races began to interact with each other. Some formed alliances and friendships, while others were wary and distrustful of those who were different. The Forest Dwellers and the Water-Bearers formed a bond over their shared connection to nature, while the Fire-Bearers and Sky-Travelers were often at odds due to their opposing elements.

Despite their differences, all Solgwais shared a deep connection to Aurue. It was the source of their power and the reason for their existence. Through it, they could communicate with each other across great distances and even control the elements themselves.

However, not all Solgwais were content to simply live in harmony with the Aurumana. There were those who sought to control it for their own gain, to use its power for their own selfish desires. These Solgwais became known as {REDACTED}, and they were feared and reviled by all other Solgwais.

The {REDACTED}, once noble and brave like their fellow Solgwais, had become twisted by their obsession with Aurue. They would stop at nothing to gain more power, even if it meant destroying their own kind. The other Solgwais banded together to stop their old brethren, but it was not an easy task. The {REDACTED} were powerful and cunning, and their reptilian scales made them nearly invincible.

A team was put together and setout on a quest to harness the power once spoke of in rituals and tales of the Dreamweavers. One Solgwais from each race to represent and lead its own, while others held off the {REDACTED} forces. Some were cunning and brave, some were quick, but all knew together they would have the best chance to reach their goal, and put past differences asides.
